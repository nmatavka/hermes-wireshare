Getting Started

Downloading

Downloading a file in WireShare is as easy as 1-2-3. Here's how:

  1. Wait for the signal bars on the bottom left of your screen to turn green.
  2. Type what you're looking for into the text field above and press Enter when you're done.
  3. Double-click on the file(s) you want to download.

Uploading

Uploading, if possible, is even easier. Simply navigate to My Files above, then click Add Files and browse to the relevant entries. Keep in mind that the files you pick will be shared publicly and anonymously; use your discretion and be sure you're complying with the law in your jurisdiction as it applies to intellectual property.
